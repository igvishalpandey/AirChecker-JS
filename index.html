About Us

Air pollution refers to the release of pollutants into the air-pollutants which are detrimental to human health and the planet as a whole. According to the World Health Organization (WHO), every year air pollution is responsible for nearly seven million deaths around the globe. Nine out of ten human beings currently breathe air that exceeds the WHO'S guideline lmits for pollutants, with those living in low and middle-income countries suffering the most. Our Job is to inform all of our clients regarding the harmful effects of air pollution and the precautions that can be taken depending on the amount of air pollution in each area.
